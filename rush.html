What is Rush?

"Rush" is a recruitment period held at the beginning of every Fall and Spring semester. It is a two-week period filled with fun, free, and non-binding events designed for you to get to know the sisters of alpha Kappa Delta Phi and learn more about our pillars: Sisterhood, Scholarship, Leadership, Service, and Asian Awareness.

There is no obligation to join by attending rush events. We encourage everyone interested to come out, meet us, and see if UNC aKDPhi is the right fit for you!

Why Rush aKDPhi?

Joining aKDPhi offers more than just a social network; it provides a support system that lasts a lifetime. As a sister, you will have opportunities to:

  • Build Lifelong Friendships: Create bonds with women who will support you through college and beyond.
  • Develop Leadership Skills: Take on positions within the chapter and attend national conferences.
  • Give Back: Participate in meaningful service projects benefiting Breast Cancer Awareness and the local community.
  • Celebrate Culture: Be part of the largest Asian-interest sorority in the nation.

Requirements & FAQ

Do I have to be Asian to join?

Not at all! While we are an Asian-interest sorority, we welcome women of all backgrounds who share our values and interest in Asian awareness.

What is the GPA requirement?

To be eligible for a bid, you must be a full-time undergraduate student at UNC Chapel Hill with a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5.

How much does it cost?

All rush events are completely free! If you are invited to join, there are semesterly dues which cover insurance, national fees, and chapter budgets.
